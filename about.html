Meet Bonnie,

Two Maltese siblings and the human who couldn't stop researching everything about them.

How it started

It began with two tiny dogs and too many questions.

When Bonnie and Bellina came home, the questions started immediately. What should they eat? How do you train two puppies at once? What's actually safe for Maltese dogs, and what's just marketing? The internet had answers — but most of them were contradictory, vague, or clearly written to sell something.

Balanced Ben Pets exists because of that frustration. Every guide here is the result of real research, real experience, and two very opinionated Maltese who have tested every theory firsthand.

The human behind it

Written by a pet owner, for pet owners.

Breno Leite is a lifelong animal lover and the founder of Balanced Ben Pets. He's not a vet — and he'll always tell you to talk to yours. What he is, is someone who has spent years learning how to give pets the best possible life, and who believes that good pet care information should be honest, clear, and free.

Every article on this site is researched, written, and reviewed by Breno personally. No AI-spun content. No copy-paste from other blogs. Just real experience, real research, and two dogs who keep life interesting.
